About Us

Innovative, Technology-driven, Disruptive and Borderless

Empowering Young Leaders for Positive Change

Get Involved →

Changemakers at the Intersection of Innovation and Global Impact

We are an exclusive global network of enterprising young professionals, aged 18 to 40, dedicated to leading and executing the JCI RISE initiative across its three fundamental pillars:

Sustaining and Rebuilding Economies

By supporting small, local businesses, we fuel economic growth.

Motivating the Workforce

We empower youth entrepreneurship, fostering innovation and productivity.

Preserving Mental Health and Well-being

We advocate for and support initiatives that prioritize mental health and wellness.

Our Core Areas of Focus encompass

  • Youth Entrepreneurship
  • Digital Economy
  • Corporate Governance
  • Social Justice
  • Personal Leadership
  • Sustainable Development

Our Vision

To become the leading global network of proactive young citizens.

Our Mission

To empower young people with development opportunities that inspire and catalyze positive change.

Tech-Driven Approach

In a constantly evolving world where technology drives progress, we are deeply committed to implementing JCI's objectives through cutting-edge technological solutions. We harness innovation to create lasting change.

What Sets Us Apart

JCI and JCI Continental Lagos members go beyond typical nonprofit work. We take the initiative to address local challenges with a global perspective. Our focus is on sustainable, long-term impact, both locally and globally. We believe that it takes just one enterprising individual to change the world. Be that enterprising leader today.
